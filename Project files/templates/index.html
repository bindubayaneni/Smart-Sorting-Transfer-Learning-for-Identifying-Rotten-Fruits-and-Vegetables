How to Keep Fruits & Vegetables Fresh

  • Store bananas, apples, and tomatoes separately as they emit ethylene gas.
  • Use airtight containers or zip-lock bags to retain freshness.
  • Refrigerate leafy greens with paper towels to absorb moisture.
  • Don't wash produce until you're ready to use it to avoid mold.

Note: Consuming spoiled fruits or vegetables may lead to food poisoning, gastrointestinal issues, or exposure to harmful mold and bacteria.
